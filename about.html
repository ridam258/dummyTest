Umesh Agarwal

The Retail Pro

His life motto is to "Work alongside retail brand owners and retailers to make them understand that the focus must always be on what consumers want, rather than what you want to sell them".

Umesh is a passionate and through Retail Professional with more than two decades of practical experience in the retail industry. In his initial years of working with large retailers, in the Indian and UAE Markets, he has closely been associated with 100 plus retail brands from all over the globe and some large retailers helping then to grow in their journey from where they were to where they wanted to be.

In his current role, he works as a Retail Business Consultant and Practitioner, imparting his industry knowledge and wisdom collected through vast experience of the industry. He helps retailers and retail brand onwers with focus on Omni Channel Growth Stratetegies, Understanding Consumer Behaviour, Data Analytics and effective implememtation of futuristic technology in the most sustainable way for growth.He is a strong proponent of creating immersive consumer experience on every shopping channel.

Originally being an Architect & Project Manager by education and Practice, he also undertakes and helps retail brand in their Retail fit out Project Management, Retail Store Design, Roll-out Management and in-store digital strategies.
UMESH is associated with multiple governing bodies of the Retail Industry in India and UAE. He actively contributes at various retail forums and participates in multiple networking events.

He is a known face in the retail industry as an author, contributor and speaker.
