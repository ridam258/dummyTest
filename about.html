Umesh Agarwal

The Retail Pro

My life motto is to "Work alongside retailers and brand owners to make them understand that the focus must always be on what consumers want, rather than what you want to sell them".

I am a passionate Retail Professional with more than two decades of practical experience in the retail industry. In my initial years of working with large retailers, in the India and UAE Markets, I have closely been associated with 100 plus retail brands from all over the globe and some large retailers helping then to grow in their journey from where they were to where they wanted to be.

In my current role, I work as a Retail Business Consultant and Practitioner, imparting my industry knowledge and wisdom collected through vast experience of the industry. I help retailers and retail brand onwers with focus on Omni Channel Growth Stratetegies, Understanding Consumer Behaviour, Data Analytics and effective implememtation of advanced technologies to build successful brands for sustainable growth.I am a strong proponent of creating immersive consumer experience on every shopping channel.

Originally being an Architect & Project Manager by education and Practice, I also undertakes and helps retail brand in their Retail fit out Project Management, Retail Store Design, Roll-out Management and in-store digital strategies.
I am associated with multiple governing bodies of the Retail Industry in India and UAE. I actively contributes at various retail forums and participates in multiple networking events.

I contribute in the retail industry as a speaker author and mentor.
